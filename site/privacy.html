Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Hustlers University UK (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses information when you visit hustlersuniversity.uk.

Hustlers University UK operates this marketing website. We do not run the education platform or process platform memberships or payments. Platform subscriptions and account data are handled by the official provider linked from our site.

We may use privacy-friendly analytics and standard cookies to understand basic usage (e.g., which pages are visited). You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

Contact details submitted via our forms are kept only as long as necessary to answer your request or as required by law. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information.

7) Your rights (UK/EEA)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data you’ve submitted via this site. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

8) Children

Our website is not directed to individuals under 13, and we do not knowingly collect children’s personal data.
